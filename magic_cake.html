magic cake

Magic Cake is one of the tastiest cakers the world has ever known. Few have tried it and lived to tell about it, and these are thir stories.

ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Pound of sugar
  • 3 Stick of butter

directions

  1. mix eggs, sugar, and butter in a large bowl
  2. Spread into a non-stick dish
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
  4. Let sit at room tempeture for 20 minutes
  5. Eat and enjoy!
